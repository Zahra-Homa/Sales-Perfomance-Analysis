#  Sales Performance Analysis Dashboard 

A compact and interactive Excel dashboard built using **PowerPivot (Data Model)**, PivotTables, and Slicers to analyze sales performance across time, product categories, customer segments, and regions.

---

##  Overview
This dashboard provides a clean, executive-friendly view of revenue, profit, discount behavior, and product performance.  
All calculations and filtering are powered by Excel’s **Data Model**, enabling fast, multi-dimensional analysis.

##  Key Features
- Interactive slicers (Year, Segment, Category, Region, Sub‑Category)  
- KPI summary: Revenue, Profit, Orders, AOV, YoY changes  
- Visualizations:  
  - Monthly Sales Trend  
  - Sales by Sub‑Category  
  - Sales by Category  
  - Discount vs Quantity  
  - Sales by Region & Segment  
- Fully dynamic, slicer-driven dashboard design

## Tools & Techniques
- Microsoft Excel

##  Insights

1. Technology leads overall revenue and generates the highest sales in 2011, 2013 and 2014. In 2012 Furniture has high sales

2. High discounts often result in negative profit
Discounts above 30–40% frequently push products into loss-making territory, suggesting a need to review discount policies.

3. Consumer segment drives most sales
The Consumer segment leads in revenue, order volume, and geographic spread.

4. Regional performance varies, West and East regions perform strongest.

5. Strong seasonality
Sales peak in November–December and dip in early Q1, following typical retail patterns.
